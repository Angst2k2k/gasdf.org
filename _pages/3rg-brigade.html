The 3rd Brigade provides resources and military support for the GA State Defense Force, GA Army National Guard and various Civilian authorities.

When ordered by the Adjutant General, we provide an organized, trained, disciplined, rapid response volunteer force to assist state and local government agencies, and civil relief organizations in impending or actual emergencies to assure the welfare and safety of the citizens of Georgia.

The Georgia State Defense Force is a legally constituted element of the Georgia Department of Defense, serving in support of the national and state constitutions under direction of the Governor and the Adjutant General of the State of Georgia.

3rd Brigade is a NEW brigade that is being fielded in the South Central part of the State. Our Soldiers will mainly imbed and train with Army Guard Units throughout our AO Area of Operation (AO). In addition to supporting various military and civilian entities in disasters, we will supply 3rd Brigade Soldiers to help train deploying Guard units.

Additionally, we field a Color Guard which assists numerous Federal, State, and Civilian organizations during ceremonies, holidays, and events.

Within the 3rd Brigade are the following units and their drill locations:

Headquarters and Headquarters Company (3rd BDE HHC, located in Americus, GA) – the administrative HQ for our personnel.

The 1st Battalion provides support to Fort Benning, GA

The 2nd Battalion provides support to the following GA Army National Guard’s C Company (Comanche), located in Cordele, GA which is part of the 2nd Battalion, 121st Infantry Regiment, 48th Infantry Brigade Combat Team:

The 3rd Battalion provides support to GA Army National Guard unit in Glennville, GA

All units support other missions when called.
