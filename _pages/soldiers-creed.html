I am a volunteer. I am a Soldier.
I stand for the citizens of Georgia.
From the majestic mountains of the north to the city of Atlanta; from the rolling plains of the south to the splendor of the sea- where I am needed is where I will be found.

Where there is despair, I will bring hope.

As a sentry on watch for my Great State, I will be ever vigilant and steadfast. I am capable, courageous and confident in my ability to defend the Constitution of the State of Georgia and the United States of America.
The citizen of Georgia can sleep peacefully, for I am ready, reliable and will respond rapidly when called.
I am a proud member of the Georgia State Defense Force.
I am Ready to Serve.
