These professional services are applied in a variety of arenas including: horizontal and vertical construction, operations and maintenance, IT, environmental remediation, civil works, medical facilities, military installations, performance venues, energy generation, commercial developments and centers of worship. Legis has been engaged on projects in the United States, Canada, Europe, Asia and Africa.
